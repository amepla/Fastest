The $10K Checklist

Eight principles from the Metics field guide — each one implemented in this interface, not just listed.

  1. 01

    Point of view, not a template

    Dark-luxury editorial direction executed without flinching — no generic bootstrap aesthetic.

  2. 02

    Typography that does work

    Fraunces display paired with Source Sans 3 body. Scale and weight carry hierarchy — nothing defaulted to Inter or Roboto.

  3. 03

    A restrained color system

    Five colors only: ink, surface, cream, copper, stone. Premium through restraint.

  4. 04

    Hierarchy that breathes

    Whitespace, scale, and contrast guide the eye — clear primary, secondary, and tertiary levels.

  5. 05

    Imagery with intent

    Custom compass-and-window SVG art direction — commissioned feel, not stock photography.

  6. 06

    Motion that whispers

    Subtle float, rise, and card hover — hand-crafted micro-interactions with reduced-motion respect.

  7. 07

    Mobile that's designed, not shrunk

    Phone layout reorders hero art, stacks content, and rescales type — not a compressed desktop.

  8. 08

    The invisible expensive stuff

    Semantic HTML, meta tags, skip link, focus states, WCAG AA contrast, and instant load.
